About Atlas

Built for role clarity, operational trust, and repeatable workflows.

AtlasBahamas is structured around one practical principle: each user should know exactly where to go next. Tenant and landlord journeys are separated at the homepage door entry, then routed through role-specific authentication.

The top toolbar remains constant across all pages through a shared shell renderer, so users always keep orientation while moving between About, Contact, Listings, Login, and dashboards.

Tenant Priorities

Rent tracking, maintenance requests, and payment history with minimal steps.

Landlord Priorities

Portfolio visibility, request status, occupancy signal, and fast task actions.
